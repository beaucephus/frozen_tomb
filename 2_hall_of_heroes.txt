====== 2 hall of heroes ======
Created Tuesday 17 March 2020

__At the base of the stairs, the hallway turns to the left and continues down another flight.__
__All along the wall you can see old bas-relief images of warriors riding some kind of wyvern, but the wyvern is depicted strangely, not flying but walking on all fours like a bear or a cat.__

__The snowy tracks continue through this hall and the scent of wet fur is heavy on the air.__
